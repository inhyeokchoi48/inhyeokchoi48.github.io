I am a postdoc at the Department of Mathematics at Cornell University. From March 2023 to June 2024, I was a postdoc at the June E Huh Center for Mathematical Challenges of KIAS, working with Prof. Sang-hyun Kim. From March to June 2024, I was also visiting the Fields Institute for the program `Thematic Program on Randomness and Geometry'. I earned my PhD in mathematics at KAIST under the supervision of Prof. Harry Hyungryul Baik. Broadly speaking, I work in geometric group theory and dynamics. These days, I am interested in random walks, growth problem and Patterson-Sullivan theory on non-positively curved spaces.
