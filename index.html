I am a postdoc at the Center for Mathematical Challenges of KIAS (Korea Institute for Advanced Study). My mentor is Prof. Sang-hyun Kim. I earned my PhD in mathematics at KAIST under the supervision of Prof. Harry Hyungryul Baik. My current research interests include:

  • Random walks on weakly hyperbolic groups and mapping class groups
  • Dynamics on Teichmüller spaces
  • Properties of (typical) pseudo-Anosov mapping classes
  • (Relatively/acylindrically/etc.) hyperbolic groups and their boundaries
  • Geometrically finite Kleinian groups and their degenerations
  • Automatic groups
